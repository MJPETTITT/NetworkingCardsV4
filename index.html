GETTING TO KNOW EACH OTHER

The goal of this exercise is to get to know your peers a little better. For this exercise, you will gather in pairs or small groups. Then follow 4 easy steps:

1. Decide who starts.

2. Click the cards to reveal two question options.

3. Ask your peer(s) one of the two questions on the cards. You can choose who answers.

4. Take turns until the networking activity is over.

TOP TIPS

Let one person share their screen, so everyone can read the questions.

Try to be short, so you can answer more questions. There is a timer to help you keep track of time. You can change the timer length yourself in the top right corner (click on the numbers).

Be flexible: adapt your question if you notice it’s difficult to answer, or move on to the next cards.
